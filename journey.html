Founder Story

My Journey: Building a Beyond Financials Lens for SME IPOs

I am NISM XIX-C and NISM XIX-E certified and deeply focused on Indian SME IPO behavior. These certifications align with AIF portfolio management competency requirements, and this page is my first-person journey from curiosity in 2022 to building a proprietary framework and dashboard that I now want to share with serious funds, banks, and institutions.

Timeline

September 2022

SME IPOs unexpectedly captured my attention. Listing gains and repeated upper circuits, especially among high-quality issuers in September and October, stood out immediately.

November 2022

While analyzing outcomes, I saw the S&P BSE SME IPO Index on TradingView. Its sharp trajectory changed my perspective and pushed me to pause my regular job to study this "big yet small" opportunity full-time.

March 2023

After nearly five months of intensive study, I made my first investment in SME stocks. With hindsight, my understanding was still at an early stage, but the learning process had truly begun.

2023 to Present

Three years later, I have built real market experience, refined a multi-factor framework, and developed a dashboard-led process to evaluate both IPO applications and post-listing investments.

Core Realizations

  • SME stocks can deliver outsized rewards, but the volatility can be extreme and unforgiving.
  • Financial metrics alone do not explain outcomes in this segment.
  • Qualitative and structural factors, including lead managers, market makers, issue design, and timing, strongly influence performance.
  • A disciplined multi-factor process paired with sound fundamentals can materially improve decision quality.
