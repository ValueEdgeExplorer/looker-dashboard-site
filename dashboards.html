Dashboards Hub

These dashboards convert years of research into an exploratory format for investors, analysts, and institutions evaluating Indian SME IPO opportunities.

Mainboard vs SME IPO Performance (2022 onward)

Why it matters: Establishes context for risk-reward asymmetry across segments.

How to use: Compare listing gains, post-listing trends, and cycle behavior.

Description: Designed to show where SMEs can outperform if selection discipline is high.

Anchor Investors Performance Dashboard (2022 onward)

Why it matters: Anchor quality often signals diligence, confidence, and issue credibility.

How to use: Filter by anchor names and examine subsequent stock behavior.

Description: Helps identify which anchor participation patterns align with stronger outcomes.

Beyond Financials Multi-Factor Dashboard (Flagship)

Why it matters: Financial metrics alone miss key SME-specific drivers.

How to use: Apply combinations of factors such as lead manager quality, issue type, anchors, seasonality, and market structure cues.

Description: My proprietary framework built from three years of research, manual validation, and pattern testing.
