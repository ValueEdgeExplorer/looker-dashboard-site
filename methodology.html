Methodology

The Beyond Financials framework combines structured data, market behavior, and qualitative context to improve signal quality in SME IPO decision-making.

Research philosophy

Financial statements provide the base layer, but outcomes in SME IPOs are heavily influenced by structural and behavioral variables. My objective is to create a practical lens for real-world allocation decisions, not just theoretical scoring.

Factor selection

  • Lead manager track record
  • Anchor participation quality and patterns
  • Issue type and structure (book-built vs fixed price)
  • Market maker behavior signals
  • Seasonality and issuance clustering effects
  • Valuation context vs pre-IPO rounds

Data sources

  • BSE/NSE disclosures and issue documents
  • Exchange data for listing and post-listing price action
  • Public anchor and issue-level information
  • Curated manual annotations from observed market behavior

Back-testing approach

I test factor combinations across cycles from 2022 onward, observe consistency in listing gain behavior and post-listing trend survival, and prioritize robustness over one-off outliers.

Limitations

  • No framework guarantees outcomes; this is a probability tool.
  • Liquidity distortions can amplify noise in small-cap segments.
  • Some factors are still handled manually and need scaled automation.
  • Past relationships may weaken under regime changes.

Upcoming features

  • Volume and post-listing participation factors
  • Expanded anchor integration modules
  • Better institutional filtering presets
  • Automated manual-factor ingestion with validation layers
